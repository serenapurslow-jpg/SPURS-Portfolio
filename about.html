👋 About Serena

Hi, I'm Serena.

A Data School Coach at The Information Lab, with a passion for Tableau! I love creating data visualisations and sharing tips and tricks with the data community. Here's my journey so far.

2016 – 2020

University of Edinburgh

My data journey started at university — a degree in International Relations with Quantitative Methods brought me into the world of statistical methods and their applications.

2022

Data School Application

In 2022 I was rung by a recruiter telling me about an amazing role with a company called The Information Lab, using a tool called Tableau. Needless to say, I was hooked.

2022 – 2025

The Information Lab — Analytics Consultant

I underwent 4 months of world-leading training in Alteryx and Tableau, and went on to develop skills in Power BI and SQL. Over three years I worked with clients across all sectors, turning data into actionable insights.

2024 – 2026

Tableau Public Ambassador

Selected as a Tableau Public Ambassador for two consecutive terms (2024–25 and 2025–26), supporting the Tableau community, helping others grow their data skills, and showcasing the power of Tableau through Tableau Public.

2025 – Present

The Information Lab — Data School Coach

My current role: coaching the next generation of Data Schoolers, helping new starters build their Tableau, Alteryx and Consulting skills from the ground up.
